Gameplay Basics for Version 1

  • The game starts with the AI.
  • The AI show you how many stones are left in the heap.
  • You can remove 1 or 2 stones from the heap on your turn.
  • You cannot remove any other number of stones.
  • You win if you remove the last 1 or 2 stones from the heap.
  • You lose if it is your turn and there are no stones left in the heap.
